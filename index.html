Natural Disasters in Southeast Asia

This page presents visualisations of natural disaster risks and the frequency of natural disasters in Southeast Asia from 2010 to 2021.

Natural Disaster Risk Map

This choropleth map visualises the overall natural disaster risk in Southeast Asian countries. The color scale indicates risk levels.

Frequency of Natural Disasters

This stacked bar chart shows the total number of natural disasters in Southeast Asian countries, along with the specific frequency of each disaster type. Use the filter to focus on individual countries.
